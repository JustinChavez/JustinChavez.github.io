HackerPack

March 2016 to Present
 HackerPack is an organization that creates software products to increase inclusivity in hackathons. I co-founded the organization along with Linhchi Nguyen. Highlights:
  • Raised $20,000 in seed funding from Keller Center's eLab accelerator in Summer 2016
  • Led a team of 6 people in business, design, and engineering on finishing Hackmates, a web application built with Django that helps people form teams for hackathons.
  • Currently preparing the application for open source adoption.
